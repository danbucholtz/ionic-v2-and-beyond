Dan Bucholtz | @danbucholtz

Ionic is a team of 25ish in Madison, WI

Founded in 2012

Make a popular tool for building software called Ionic Framework

We're well funded financially and will be around for many years to come

Two versions, 1 and 2

Ionic Framework is a free, open source solution to build Android and iOS "Hybrid" applications

Look and feel of a "native" app, but one code set for both platforms

Unlock the full functionality of the device using Cordova (Phone Gap)

Just HTML, CSS, JS!

Ionic uses Sass for "better css"

Sass is easier to customize to fit your brand

Built on top of AngularJS

Great performance

Empower developers everywhere

"If you can make a website, you can make an app"!

Next-gen of Ionic is even simpler!
